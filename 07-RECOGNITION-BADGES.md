# Recognition & Badge System

## Badge Tier Structure

### Tier 1 — Peak Leadership & Long-Term Milestones
- Operations Director badge
- 5-Year service milestone
- 10-Year service milestone
- 15-Year service milestone
- Comes with the biggest celebrations, wall displays, and special pins.

### Tier 2 — Role & Position Badges
- Trainer Path badge
- Station Specialist badge
- Team Leader stages
- Specialty Director badge
- Legacy Builder (2+ years of consistent high impact)
- Recognizes professional growth and advancement.

### Tier 3 — Core Journey & Bonus Recognition
- Core journey completion badges (1st, 2nd, 3rd certification milestones)
- Peak Performance Hero — Calm excellence during peak times
- Above & Beyond — Exceptional initiative
- Values Champion — Embodies Chick-fil-A values daily
- Celebrates daily excellence and special contributions.

---

## Multi-Year Milestone Recognition

| Years of Service | Milestone Name | Celebration |
|-----------------|----------------|-------------|
| 5 Years | 5-Year Milestone | Team celebration + metallic badge |
| 10 Years | 10-Year Milestone | Commemorative pin + special recognition event |
| 15 Years | 15-Year Milestone | Legacy celebration, wall plaque, and evergreen badge |

---

## Personal Achievement Badges

These recognize individual strengths regardless of which path a team member is on:

- **Single-Station Mastery** — Mastery of first station and consistent reliability.
- **Dual-Station Mastery** — Mastery of two stations and growing versatility.
- **Steadfast Contributor** — Exceptional consistency and guest care in any role.
- **Heart of Hospitality** — Warmth and service that guests rave about.
- **Determination Award** — Overcoming personal challenges to contribute every day.

---

## Badge Implementation Notes

- Present badges as soon as achievements are verified (immediate recognition).
- Higher tiers warrant team announcements and public displays.
- Track badge progress in team member files for performance reviews.
- Encourage team members to nominate each other for bonus/recognition badges.
- Badge progress is tracked in each team member's personal record.
