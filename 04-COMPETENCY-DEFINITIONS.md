# Competency Definitions

## Leadership Circle Profile — Creative Dimensions

These are the 12 competencies from the Leadership Circle Profile 360, organized by dimension. All 12 are assigned to tiers in the competency runway. Each includes the assessment questions used in the 360, which also serve as behavioral indicators.

### Relating

**Caring** (assigned to: TM + Trainer)
- I connect deeply with others.
- I am compassionate.
- I form warm and caring relationships.

**Mentoring** (assigned to: Asst Director)
- I help Direct Reports create development plans.
- I am a people builder/developer.
- I provide feedback focused on professional growth.
- I help people learn, improve, and change.

**Teamwork** (assigned to: Trainer)
- I create a positive climate that supports people doing their best.
- I promote high levels of teamwork through my leadership style.
- I share leadership.

**Interpersonal** (assigned to: Team Leader)
- I learn from mistakes.
- I take responsibility for my part of relationship problems.
- In a conflict, I accurately restate the opinions of others.
- I listen openly to criticism and ask questions to further understand.

### Being

**Balance** (assigned to: Director)
- I balance work and personal life.
- I find enough time for personal reflection.

**Composure** (assigned to: Team Leader)
- I am composed under pressure.
- I am a calming influence in difficult situations.
- I handle stress and pressure very well.

**Integrity** (assigned to: Asst Director)
- I am a good role model for the vision I espouse.
- I exhibit personal behavior consistent with my values.
- I hold to my values during good and bad times.
- I lead in a manner that is completely aligned with my values.

**Courage** (assigned to: Team Leader)
- I surface the issues others are reluctant to talk about.
- I speak directly even on controversial issues.
- I am courageous in meetings.

### Achieving

**Vision** (assigned to: Director)
- I communicate a compelling vision.
- I inspire others with vision.
- I provide strategic vision for the organization.
- I articulate a vision that creates alignment within the organization.

**Strategy** (assigned to: Director)
- I have a firm grasp of the market place dynamics.
- I provide strategic direction that is thoroughly thought through.
- I am a gifted strategist.
- I establish a strategic direction that helps the organization to thrive.

**Results** (assigned to: Asst Director)
- I am quick to seize opportunities upon noticing them.
- I pursue results with drive and energy.
- I am proficient at achieving high quality results on key initiatives.
- I strive for continuous improvement.

**Decisions** (assigned to: Asst Director)
- I am an efficient decision maker.
- I make the tough decisions when required.
- I make decisions in a timely manner.

## Foundational Competency (Not in Leadership Circle)

**Coachability** (assigned to: TM)
- Receives feedback without defensiveness or excuses.
- Applies feedback visibly — you can see the change in the next shift.
- Asks questions to understand, not to argue.
- Seeks feedback proactively — doesn't wait for it to come to them.

## Operator-Level Competencies (Beyond Leadership Circle)

Derived from coaching conversations about the operator role, the 6 essential jobs of the operator, and the CFA 5 Critical Success Factors.

**Stewardship** (assigned to: Operator)
- Being a faithful owner of what's been entrusted to you.
- Doing the essential work that only you can do — the 6 core jobs, the anchored blocks.
- Not just running a business, but tending to it responsibly.

**Scalability** (assigned to: Operator)
- Your leadership multiplies without your presence.
- You've built leaders who build leaders.
- The restaurant runs — and the culture holds — when you're not there.
- Test: "If you took your least available month, would you still be a good steward?"

**Legacy** (assigned to: Operator)
- The culture you're shaping outlasts any individual.
- The hospitality ethos, the development paths, the "how we made people feel" piece.
- Long-term investment in people and systems that endure.

## Leadership Circle — Reactive Dimensions (Reference Only)

These are not assigned to tiers but are useful for awareness — patterns that can limit leadership effectiveness.

### Controlling
- **Perfectionist** — Need to perform flawlessly, excel in every situation.
- **Ambitious** — Believe winning is what really matters, excessively ambitious.
- **Autocratic** — Domineering, dictate rather than influence.

### Protecting
- **Arrogant** — Self-centered, too big of an ego.
- **Critical** — Hurt people's feelings, sarcastic and/or cynical.
- **Distant** — Emotionally distant, hard to get to know, aloof.

### Complying
- **Passive** — Lack passion, wishy-washy in decision making.
- **Belonging** — Adopt others' views to avoid disappointing them, play it too safe.
- **Pleasing** — Worry about others' judgment, need approval.
