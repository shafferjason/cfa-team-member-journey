# CFA Wall Street — Team Member Journey & Career Progression Framework

## What This Is

We're building the complete team member development and career progression framework for Chick-fil-A Wall Street — the system that defines how someone goes from their first day as a new hire all the way through to director-level leadership and beyond. It's a dual-track model where every tier has both operational skills they must master and leadership competencies they must develop before advancing. The competencies are drawn from the Leadership Circle Profile 360 assessment, assigned deliberately so that earlier tiers focus on relational foundations (Caring, Teamwork) and later tiers build toward strategic leadership (Vision, Strategy, Balance). The coach calls it the "runway" — each tier has a minimum time requirement and clear graduation criteria so people know exactly where they are, what they need to grow in, and what's next. The goal is a system where every team member — whether they want to become a director or stay as a valued long-term contributor — has a clear, respected path with recognition and development built in.

## Guiding Anchors

- **Vision:** Create effortless experiences through intentional care.
- **Leadership Directive:** Everyone feels confident and cared for — our team members and our guests.
- **CFA 5 Critical Success Factors:** Leadership → Talent → Customer Experience → Sales & Brand Growth → Financial Stewardship
